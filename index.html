MAS-AIxIA

WG on Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems

The AIxIA working group on Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent (MAS-AIxIA) coordinates the activities of a group of Italian researchers working in the area of agent-oriented technologies.
The group promotes the organisation of relevant events (in particular, the annual national workshop “From Objects to Agents”, that will reach the 26th edition in 2025), and support tools (such as a mailing-list and a website of the group).
